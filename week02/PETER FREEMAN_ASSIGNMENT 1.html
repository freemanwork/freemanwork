Purpose

- This site is meant to show my digital and print work in a manner that establishes my identity as a graphic designer

- I would like the interface to show my ability to translate ideas and concepts into simple and visually appealing pieces

- This site should encourage exploration, yet be simple and easy to manage

- Another piece I would like to include would be small sections that show my hobbies (like making zines)

Audience

- The audience that I am primarily trying to make this site for involves employers. I would think that they should be able to explore the site and learn about me and what I can make.

- I would also like this site to be a comfortable piece to show friends what I am working on and potentially how they could be involved on current projects.

Tone

I would like to keep this website to maintain a concious, yet casual tone. I think the clients that I would like to have should understand who I am as a person and see that I have a personality outside of simply my work.
